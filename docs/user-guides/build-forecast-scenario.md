# Build a Forecast Scenario

This guide explains how to create forecast scenarios to evaluate
different business outcomes under varying assumptions.

Forecast scenarios help teams model uncertainty and support
data-driven decision-making.

---

## Step 1: Create a new forecast version

1. Navigate to **Planning > Forecasts**
2. Select **Create New Version**
3. Choose **Scenario Forecast**
4. Enter a name and description

Use clear naming to distinguish scenarios (for example, Best Case or Conservative).

---

## Step 2: Select a baseline

Choose a starting point:
- Latest approved forecast
- Approved budget
- Actuals-to-date

Baselines ensure consistency across scenarios.

---

## Step 3: Apply scenario-specific assumptions

Adjust assumptions such as:
- Revenue growth rates
- Headcount changes
- Cost increase or reduction

Assumptions can be applied globally or at a granular level.

---

## Step 4: Review AI-assisted insights

AI insights may:
- Highlight unusual variances
- Surface potential forecast risks
- Identify areas that require closer review

Insights are advisory and should be reviewed alongside business context.

---

## Step 5: Save and validate

Save the scenario and review validation messages.
Resolve any issues before proceeding.

---

## Next steps

After creating scenarios, review results and submit the
selected forecast for approval.
