# Review and Submit a Forecast

This guide explains how to review forecast results and
submit a forecast for approval.

---

## Step 1: Review forecast summaries

Review key metrics such as:
- Total revenue
- Expense trends
- Variance from prior versions

Use summary views to identify areas requiring attention.

---

## Step 2: Evaluate AI insights and warnings

AI insights and warnings may highlight:
- Large variances
- Confidence indicators
- Potential forecast risks

Address these items before submission.

---

## Step 3: Add review comments

Add contextual comments to:
- Explain assumptions
- Capture decision rationale
- Highlight risks or dependencies

Comments improve collaboration and audit readiness.

---

## Step 4: Submit for approval

1. Select **Submit for Approval**
2. Review submission details
3. Confirm approval workflow participants

After submission, editing may be restricted.

---

## Step 5: Track approval status

Track approval progress directly within the forecast.
Notifications alert you when action is required.

---

## What happens next

Once approved, the forecast can be:
- Locked for reporting
- Used in executive dashboards
- Compared against actuals
