# Frequently Asked Questions

This section answers common questions related to forecasting, budgeting, and AI-assisted insights.

---

## Forecasting

### Can I create multiple forecasts for the same period?
Yes. You can create multiple forecast versions to model different scenarios and assumptions.

### Can forecasts be locked?
Yes. Forecasts can be locked after submission to prevent further changes.

---

## AI-assisted insights

### Does AI change my data automatically?
No. AI insights are advisory only. They highlight patterns and anomalies but do not modify values.

### How accurate are AI recommendations?
AI insights are based on historical data patterns and should be reviewed alongside business context.

---

## Collaboration and governance

### Can multiple users work on the same forecast?
Yes. Forecasting supports collaboration with role-based access and approval workflows.

### Is there an audit trail?
Yes. All changes are tracked with user, timestamp, and value history.

---

## Performance

### How does the platform handle large datasets?
The platform is optimized for enterprise-scale data volumes and supports efficient recalculation and aggregation.
